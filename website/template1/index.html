Roasted Pumpkin Seeds

  1. Scrape out your seeds.
  2. Rinse the pulp.
  3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast at 300° for 30 minutes.
  4. Remove and toss with 2 tablespoons of butter, a fat pinch of salt and some ground pepper.
  5. Return to oven for an additional 20 minutes.
  6. Feast.

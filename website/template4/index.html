• Festival •

For many of you Tropicana Clubbers, Club Tropicana doesn't need an introduction. For the other Club Tropicana rookies: scroll through these pages, look at the movie, look at the pictures, and get a taste of the most exciting, the most smashing and the most crazy Hockey tournament of this continent.

This year we are pushing it a little further: Club Tropicana is now also a music festival. Our line-up this year will bring you a mix of quality and party music. So if you want to register a team, get your crew together and register quick (last edition we sold out in 2 weeks). If you just want to party and enjoy the vibe grab your tickets on this site!

• Tournament •

Of course, Hockey remains the main activity of this event. Whether you consider hockey to be an excuse for partying or the main reason why you come down to Mechelen Beach, is totally up to you.

But the rules of the game have to be clear.
Indoor rules
You need 10 players for one team (1 goalie)
Per team minimum 3 girls
All players must be older then 18

"Tournament is from Friday evening till Saturday evening. So first games start at 20H00 on Friday and last until Saturday 19H.
No games on Sunday."

• Leroy & Tibson •

Time schedule tba

Leroy & Tibson are Corneel Leroy and Tibbe van Hoof, two young guys from Mechelen (Belgium) with a passion for quality music and/on vinyl.

Their DJ-sets are strictly vinyl and predominantly house oriented, interspersed with some disco, funk, boogie, italo, old school hip hop and fresh 80’s sounds.

Together with Le Curateur, they are the hosts of 'Slave To The Rhythm', every Thursday at AperO (Leuven).

Their first joint mixtape 'Coming Out' was selected as 'Fuzzbox mixtape of the month’ by Fuzz Magazine.

Leroy & Tibson have been guests on We Play House Recordings @ Decadance twice, on Switch (Studio Brussel), played at Tomorrowland (Dreamville Radio, Studio Brussel), Ostend Beach and Café d'Anvers, and already shared flyers with Compuphonic, Raoul Lambert, Red D, San Soda and many others.

• Hermanos Inglesos •

Time schedule tba

For the first time ever at Club Tropicana, the brothers Engels aka Hermanos Inglesos. Most of you will know these brothers, as they have been performing at the biggest Belgian parties for many years now.

After their debut album in 2010 album ‘The Wander of You’, with the singles ‘Mastermind’ (a favourite of Erol Alkan, Fake Blood and Tiga) and ‘Wanderland’ (received airplay on BBC Radio 1, the poignant animation video got more than two million views), they will release their second EP “Hermanos Inglesos” this year.

The single ‘The Fastlane’ is the premium example of that new-found confidence: a poppy clubtrack with soulful vocals and beats, that can be stripped down to an equally strong acoustic song. Lead vocals are by Anne Freier, a German singer who Cedric met in London. Meanwhile the brothers have also remixed Hooverphonic’s ‘Amalfi’.

Time schedule tba

Lexer will be playing his first set in Belgium at Club Tropicana. He is one of the favourites DJs from the Club Tropicana crew. Simply great music!

It's very tough to present his music in his home area. It's a new style of deep and menlancolic pop music and everyone is touched by his special way to produce and play this music. • Dave Lambert •

Time schedule tba

Dave Lambert is without a doubt one of the most influential DJs in the Belgian HOUSE scene. Most importantly this will be his 6th appearance at Club Tropicana, and therefore we consider him to be the Club Tropicana resident DJ. Many of the Tropicana Clubbers remember his legendary sets that lasted till the early morning….simply legendary. Besides playing at all the big national events like Sensation White, Tomorrowland, Cityparade, Laundry Day, Groovecity and many more, he also was resident at venues like VERSUZ and ZILLION. Internationally Dave Lambert is no stranger with gigs in countries and cities such as Japan, Miami, Dubai, Spain, Ibiza, Croatia, Germany and several others! He has remixed records for Fedde Le Grand, Benny Benassi, Mason, John Dahlback, Wally Lopez, Thomas Gold, Ida Corr and loads more.
